Cellular Systems: Multiple Access and Interference Management

Introduction

2 types of spectra:

  • licensed（FM:100MHz GSM:900MHz 3G 2GHz）（nationwide and over a period of a few years, provide immunity from any kind of interference outside of the system itself, but expensive bandwidth, no power constraints）
  • unlicensed(for experimental systems and to aid development of new wireless tech, cheap bandwidth, a maximum power constraint as well as interference)
